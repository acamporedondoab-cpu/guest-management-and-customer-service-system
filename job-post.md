**Automation & Systems Architect (GoHighLevel, Make.com, Databases, APIs)**

We are building a guest management and revenue intelligence platform for RV parks, campgrounds, and outdoor hospitality operators throughout the United States.

Our technology stack includes:

* GoHighLevel (CRM)
* Make.com (automation layer)
* Supabase (database layer)
* Reservation System APIs and Webhooks
* AI-powered communication tools
* Reporting and analytics systems

We are seeking a highly technical systems builder who can help design, build, and maintain the infrastructure that powers the platform.
